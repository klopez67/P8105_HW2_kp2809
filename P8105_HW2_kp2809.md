Convert the entry variable from character (YES vs NO) to a logical
variable (the ifelse or case_match function may be useful).

``` r
transit_tidy_df= 
  transit_df|>
  janitor::clean_names() |>
  select( line:ada, -(staffing),-(staff_hours), -(exit_only) ) |>
  mutate(
  entry = ifelse(entry =="YES", "TRUE","FALSE"))
```
